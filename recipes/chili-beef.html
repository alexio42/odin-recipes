Chili Beef

Description

Delicious chili beef recipe. Deep fry beef in wok for crispy beef. Adjust heat by using more chilis or adding chili oil at the end.

Ingredients

  • Beef, thinly sliced
  • Spring onion, cut into 4-5cm pieces
  • Handful of coriander, roughly chopped
  • 1tsp sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Potato starch
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Oyster sauce
  • Chilies, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine the beef, sugar, a little soy sauce, a little oil, a small amont of potato starch and lots of freshly ground black pepper. Cover and leave to marinate.
  2. Heat some oil in a wok/pan until hot and fry beef in batches until they have some colour but are still pink in places - a couple of minutes. Remove all beef from wok when done.
  3. Add more oil and heat if necessary, then add spring onion, coriander, chili, and garlic. After a couple of minutes, when spring onion is starting to soften, add all of the beef back into the wok and season with soy sauce, oyster sauce and more sugar if needed.
