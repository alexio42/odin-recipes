Hainan Chicken

Description

Fragrant poached chicken recipe, often served with green sauce and chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Cloves from whole bulb garlic, peeled and crushed
  • Spring onion
  • Ginger, chunk, chopped
  • Chicken stock
  • Chinese cooking wine
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Take a pan large enough to hold the chicken and pour in 1 part stock to 2 parts water - enough to cover the chicken - and place it on the heat.
  2. Take the spring onion, cut off the green parts, and tie them in a knot. Add them to the pan with the garlic.
  3. While the stock/water is heating, chop up the white parts of the spring onion and put into the chicken cavity, with some chinese cooking wine and the ginger. Trim excess fat and skin from the chicken.
  4. When the stock/water is boiling, place the chicken into it and boil for a minute, then turn off the heat and leave covered.
  5. After 45 minutes, turn on the heat again for half an hour.
  6. Take the chicken out, remove the stuffing from cavity and splash some soy sauce and sesame oil over the chicken while it's still warm.
