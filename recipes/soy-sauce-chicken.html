Description

Fairly simply soy sauce chicken recipe. Can be made with a whole chicken, cut in half quarters or portions. Or buy a pack of skin on bone in chicken thighs and drumsticks.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken, cut into pieces, or pack of drumsticks/thighs
  • Chunk of ginger, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • Vegetable oil
  • Star anise, broken into pieces
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Chinese cooking wine
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Dry the chicken skin with kitchen paper ad heat oil in a pan large enough for all the chicken.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger to the hot oil for a minute or two, then add the chicken, skin side down.
  3. When skin is browned, turn and brown other side, then add rest of ingredients to pan. Cover pan and leave on low heat.
  4. After half an hour (or when chicken cooked through), remove chicken from pan and turn up heat to reduce liquid.
  5. Cut up chicken (if necessary), and coat in reduced liquid. Serve.
